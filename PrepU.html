PREPU

Duration : 26 Weeks (To Present)
Tools: Miro, Figma
Strategies : UX/UI Design, Front-End Development
Team : Abby Lui, Carol Chen, Mengchan Zhao, Moneet Tiwana, Anito So

INTRODUCTION

PrepU is a web app that satisfies the market for an accessible and effective job interview preparation program to help develop compelling communication skills and advance career goals. This project was made possible with the help of the ADA Mentorship program, ran with Women in Tech Society (WITS) to help students develop a technical project with the help of professional developers.


START AN INTERVIEW


Customize features, view statistics, and provide feedback on the homepage. To start your interview, set your settings and view your assessment on the side as your present.




THE INSIGHTS

The user will be shown a detailed summary of feedback based on the interview. They will also see the level of enthusiasm, number of filler words, number of repeated words, and pause time, and when exactly it happened during the interview. On the performance page, the user could compare view their past interview feedback.


THE PROBLEM

"How can we offer a product that allows job applicants to effectively prepare for job interviews?"

Online interviews are increasingly prevalent, especially due to COVID​

It is difficult to receive feedback from pre-recorded online interviews​

There is a lack of free end-user facing online resources available for students​

THE RESEARCH


To start off, I created an in depth competative analysis with buckets based on the features of our competitors. I noticed that the competitors had directed their product for employers and agencies, and there was a lack of products for personal use to improve interview performance in the market.


We then conducted a survey with a focus group of university students for feedback on supportive features.

STRATEGY & PLANNING

Using Miro, we first created clusters based on features we would implement. We then created a user journey based on our persona:

"Undergraduates or recent grads of any gender or age who come out of highschool/university/college with limited experience with job interviews. They have a busy schedule and want a flexible training program to further develop their communications skills. It is important for kick-starting their career and starting off strong."

We then designed a user flow displaying each page of our design and obtain feedback from our mentors and development team.


DESIGN

On figma. we created a mood board and chose our color palette​



We then created a high fidelity wireframe and standardized style guide,.

THE SOLUTION

SIMULATION
•     Available anytime, any day​
•     Fully customizable according to users' specific needs​

ANALYSIS
•     Measures and quantifies users' enthusiasm, filler and repeated words, as well as pause time​

FEEDBACK
•     Real-time insights allow users to quickly improve​
•     Tracks user progress and generates performance reports​ ​

FINAL

Through this project, learnt the importance of feasibility and considering a potential solution. We have to learn to determine which areas of the design would be capable of being developed within the timeframe of the project.
Overall, this was a fun project to work on and it felt good to see what we had accomplished together in the past few months. In March 2021, our project was announced as the winner of this year's ADA Mentorship program, with the best overall techcnical implementation and user interface design.
