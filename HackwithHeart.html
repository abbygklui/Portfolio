Hack with Heart - Women in Tech Society

Initiative Graphics

Duration : November 8, 2020 - November 11, 2020

Type : Sole Designer

Responsibilities : Graphic Design

Tools : Adobe Illustrator, Figma

About this Project


Hack with Heart is a program ran by Women in Tech Society (WITS) to help students develop projects that solve real-world problems. Although this program does not launch until January 2021, WITS has created learning tracks for students interested in Hack with Heart to acquire basic skills to help develop the project. These tracks were split into two divisions, the Product Design & Software Engineering track, as well as the Artificial Intelligence track which collaborates with Western AI. These educational opportunities take place from November to January.

The Process


To produce stronger designs, I first thought of different words that related to the three fields, product design, software engineering, and artificial intelligence. Then, I drew some illustrations some drawings based on these words. I developed a style that would be consistent for all of the Hack with Heart graphics in the future, while keeping the characteristics of WITS graphics in mind. Since this was a new event for WITS, I had more opportunities to explore the topics given to me.

My Reflection


As this was a new program, I learnt how to independently create a style for Hack with Hearts that stays consistent to WITS branding and graphics. I used a consistent style throughout my work. Since I had a limited amount of time to work on these designs, I focused on getting the message across through both the graphics and text. Overall, I am proud of what I was able to make within a short timeframe.
